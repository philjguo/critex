In this era driven by Artificial Intelligence (AI), we bring you a story about technology, ethics, and the future. "The Choices of the Future" is not just the story of Eva, a tech entrepreneur, but also a story about each of us facing the challenges brought by AI. Through Eva's decisions, we explore the delicate relationship between technological progress and humanity, as well as the moral responsibilities we should not ignore in our pursuit of efficiency and innovation.

We hope this story will inspire professionals and enthusiasts in the tech industry to think:

How do we balance the power of technology with our core human values as we continue to move forward? How should we make wise and visionary decisions with the help of AI? Standing at the crossroads of human history, how should we choose?

"Looking at Prometheus's proposal for automation, I inevitably feel apprehensive. Its logic and efficiency are impeccable, but the idea of machines replacing human labor makes me ponder deeply. I believe Prometheus can find the most optimized path, even though it means short-term unemployment issues. Perhaps, this is a necessary step toward a more efficient future.”

Trust AI: Eva decides to reshape the economic structure according to Prometheus's recommendations, leading to a massive outflow of capital, a collapse in investor confidence, and a stock market crash. The affluent and businesses begin to resist, increasing political instability. Although poverty is somewhat alleviated, the entire economic system plunges into chaos, leading to a long-term economic recession.

Trust Human: Choosing gradual reform, Eva finds the pace too slow to keep up with the ever-widening gap between rich and poor. Societal discontent continues to rise, and the impoverished feel forgotten and marginalized. Long-term social unrest and poverty issues lead to increased crime rates and the collapse of societal structures.

"When I examine Prometheus's proposal for wealth redistribution, I can't deny its calm and precise reasoning. This plan might overturn the existing economic structure, but the data and predictions it's based on convince me. It's a bold step, but I believe in AI's analytical capabilities. It might lead us toward a more equal future, even though this path is filled with uncertainties.”

"Standing in this AI-driven classroom, traditional teaching methods can no longer keep up with the pace of development. The personalized learning plan offered by AI is more efficient. I am confident in Prometheus's ability to make educational decisions, providing the most suitable education for each child. However, I'm also contemplating how to maintain the guidance and emotional connection of human teachers in this process.”

Tust AI: Eva pushes for an educational revolution, with AI taking the lead. Students receive highly standardized education but gradually lose their creative thinking and social interaction skills. The human element in education weakens, and students become more like machines, lacking the ability to deal with the complexities of the real world.

Tust Human: Persisting with human-centric education, Eva realizes that students are falling behind in adapting to a rapidly developing technological environment. As the world increasingly relies on AI and digital technology, students lack the necessary technical skills and knowledge, struggling to find their footing in a competitive job market, leading to talent gaps and employment difficulties.

In a future driven by artificial intelligence, how do we find a balance between the efficiency and indifference of technology? When faced with solutions proposed by AI, how do we measure and protect those qualities that make us human? How do we ensure that we do not lose the core of our humanity on the path to technological innovation? This question is not only a reflection on Eva's story but also a deep thought for everyone in the tech industry and tech enthusiasts in the real world. As AI increasingly intervenes in every corner of our lives, how do we make decisions that both promote social progress and uphold human dignity and ethics?
